How I keep teams aligned

Evidence before opinion

Hypotheses start with real voices—shadowing clinicians, interviewing customers, unpacking data exhaust—so the roadmap reflects actual needs.

Compliance-ready ops

From HIPAA reviews to model governance, I build rituals that keep design, legal, and engineering moving in lockstep.

Inclusive facilitation

Workshops and rituals that bring product, research, engineering, and go-to-market partners into the same story—even across time zones.

Metrics that matter

Success is codified through dashboards and OKRs: access, satisfaction, adoption, and the operational efficiencies leaders crave.
